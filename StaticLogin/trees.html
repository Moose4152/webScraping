Types of Trees

Oak: Found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. Known for its strength and longevity.

Banyan: Native to India and tropical Asia. Famous for its aerial roots and large canopy.

Maple: Common in North America, Europe, and Asia. Known for its beautiful fall foliage and maple syrup.

Neem: Native to the Indian subcontinent. Widely used in traditional medicine.

Redwood: Found along the coast of California, USA. Among the tallest trees in the world.
